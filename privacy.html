Who We Are

PropertyFilmsTO is a sole proprietorship operated by an individual filmmaker based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. We provide real estate photography, videography, and drone services to clients across the Greater Toronto Area.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

What We Collect

When you fill out our contact form or book a call, we may collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your professional role (e.g. realtor, developer, stager)
  • Property address related to your inquiry
  • Any information you include in your message

When you visit our website, we also collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics, including pages visited, time on site, and general location (city/country level). This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.

Why We Collect It

We collect your information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about our services
  • Schedule and confirm calls or shoots
  • Send you quotes, invoices, and project files related to your booking
  • Improve our website through anonymous analytics data

We will only contact you for purposes directly related to your inquiry. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.

Your Consent

By submitting our contact form, you provide express consent for PropertyFilmsTO to contact you in connection with your inquiry. This consent satisfies the requirements of CASL for commercial electronic messages sent in response to your request.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by emailing us at hello@propertyfilmsto.ca with the subject line "Unsubscribe" or "Remove my data."

Who We Share It With

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party.

We use the following third-party tools that may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Formspree — for processing contact form submissions. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Calendly — for scheduling calls. Calendly Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — for anonymous website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Zoho Mail — for email communication. Zoho Privacy Policy

We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process in Canada.

How We Protect It

Your information is stored in password-protected accounts with Zoho Mail, Formspree, and Calendly. We do not store contact form submissions on our own servers — all inquiries are processed by Formspree and delivered directly to our email inbox.

Our website uses SSL encryption (HTTPS) to protect data in transit between your browser and our site.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies set by Google Analytics to collect anonymous visitor data. These cookies do not store personally identifiable information.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use the site. You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Your Rights Under PIPEDA

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent to be contacted at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
